It all started in 2016 on Whatsapp. I had a few groups, managed by a few admins, who updated the Load Shedding schedules to the different groups. I then switched to Telegram Groups and Channels, which was updated by software. This page spread from the small number of Telegram groups that grew to a few hundered. As more and more people joined, new areas were requested and a search facility was an obvious progression. All areas in South Africa are now covered.
